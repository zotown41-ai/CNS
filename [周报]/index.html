16-h fasting optimizes cancer immunotherapy in mice and humans

  • 期刊 / 日期：Cell Metabolism / 2026-02-19 (online ahead of print)
  • 研究问题：Can a short and clinically tolerable fasting window improve cancer immunotherapy without requiring extreme dietary restriction?
  • 模型 / 队列：Mouse tumor models plus translational human neoadjuvant immunotherapy samples.
  • 关键方法：Tumor and immune-cell metabolic profiling, intratumoral nutrient analysis, and immune-state readouts.
  • 主要内容：The study tests a 16-hour fasting regimen and asks whether it changes nutrient competition inside tumors rather than only systemic metabolism.
  • 核心发现：Short fasting increased intratumoral isoleucine availability and supported CD8 T cell epigenetic and membrane-lipid reprogramming, improving immunotherapy response.
  • 研究思路：

1. Define a fasting window that is plausible for clinical use.

2. Measure how the intervention changes nutrient flow in the tumor microenvironment.

3. Link those metabolic changes to stronger CD8 T cell programs and better treatment response.

  • 转化意义：This is one of the clearest examples of a low-cost behavioral intervention being positioned as a combination partner for immunotherapy.
  • 局限与下一步：Generalizability across tumor types, baseline nutritional states, and treatment regimens still needs prospective clinical testing.
  • 链接：https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41720105/

Ketogenic diet alleviates septic lung injury via microbial gut-lung axis

  • 期刊 / 日期：Cell Metabolism / 2026-03-03
  • 研究问题：How does a ketogenic diet reduce septic lung injury, and is the effect mediated by host metabolism alone or by the gut microbiota as well?
  • 模型 / 队列：Mouse sepsis models with supporting human microbiome and clinical correlation data.
  • 关键方法：Diet intervention, microbiome profiling, metabolite tracing, and lung immune-cell analysis.
  • 主要内容：The paper connects a ketogenic state to enrichment of specific Lactobacillus strains that convert oleic acid into azelaic acid, which then acts in the lung.
  • 核心发现：A gut-microbe-derived metabolite promoted neutrophil apoptosis, expanded MerTK+ alveolar macrophages, and improved lung injury outcomes.
  • 研究思路：

1. Observe how ketogenic feeding reshapes the gut microbiota.

2. Identify the key microbial metabolic step and its product.

3. Show that the metabolite reaches the lung and reprograms local immune repair.

  • 转化意义：It offers a full diet-microbe-metabolite-organ mechanism that may be more actionable than descriptive microbiome association studies.
  • 局限与下一步：Safety, feasibility, and timing of ketogenic intervention in heterogeneous septic patients remain major translational questions.
  • 链接：https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41643678/

Targeting amyloid-β pathology by chimeric antigen receptor astrocyte (CAR-A) therapy

  • 期刊 / 日期：Science / 2026-03-05
  • 研究问题：Can astrocytes be engineered with CAR-like receptors to directly attack amyloid-β pathology in Alzheimer's disease?
  • 模型 / 队列：Engineered astrocyte systems and in vivo Alzheimer's disease models.
  • 关键方法：Cell engineering, in vivo plaque analysis, and single-nucleus RNA sequencing.
  • 主要内容：The study ports CAR logic from immunotherapy into the brain by turning astrocytes into plaque-targeting therapeutic cells.
  • 核心发现：Two CAR-A designs reduced amyloid plaque burden and induced coordinated astrocyte-microglia responses in vivo.
  • 研究思路：

1. Build amyloid-targeting CAR constructs in astrocytes.

2. Test whether the engineered cells reduce established or emerging plaque pathology in vivo.

3. Use single-cell or single-nucleus profiling to define how glial networks are remodeled after treatment.

  • 转化意义：The paper expands cell therapy into neurodegeneration and suggests that engineered resident-support cells may become therapeutic effectors.
  • 局限与下一步：Delivery, persistence, inflammation control, and safety in the human brain remain open barriers to translation.
  • 链接：https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41785364/

Genetically encoded fluorescent reporters to visualize α-synuclein pathology in live brain

  • 期刊 / 日期：Cell / 2026-03-04 (online ahead of print)
  • 研究问题：Can alpha-synuclein aggregation and spread be visualized longitudinally in living brain tissue with genetically encoded reporters?
  • 模型 / 队列：Reporter systems and knock-in mice with live-brain imaging and functional readouts.
  • 关键方法：Reporter engineering, in vivo imaging, calcium imaging, electrophysiology, and single-cell analyses.
  • 主要内容：The authors created fluorescent reporters that brighten when incorporated into alpha-synuclein inclusions, enabling longitudinal observation in awake mice.
  • 核心发现：The system makes it possible to track pathology spread while coupling aggregate formation to neuronal activity and downstream functional change.
  • 研究思路：

1. Engineer reporters that selectively respond to alpha-synuclein inclusions.

2. Validate that reporter signal tracks pathology propagation in living brain.

3. Combine pathology imaging with physiology and omics to study consequence rather than only presence.

  • 转化意义：This is a high-value tool platform that can accelerate mechanism studies, drug screening, and cell-type vulnerability mapping in synucleinopathies.
  • 局限与下一步：The platform still needs broader validation across disease stages and different aggregate conformations.
  • 链接：https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41785852/

The ubiquitin ligase KLHL6 drives resistance to CD8+ T cell dysfunction

  • 期刊 / 日期：Nature / 2026-01-14
  • 研究问题：Which protein-level regulators simultaneously protect CD8 T cells from terminal dysfunction and maintain mitochondrial fitness?
  • 模型 / 队列：In vivo CRISPR screening with tumor and chronic infection contexts.
  • 关键方法：Functional genomics, protein-degradation analysis, and mitochondrial-state assays.
  • 主要内容：The paper moves beyond transcriptional descriptions of exhaustion and identifies KLHL6 as a regulator connecting proteostasis to functional persistence.
  • 核心发现：KLHL6 constrained terminal dysfunction, supported mitochondrial fitness, and improved anti-tumor or antiviral performance when maintained.
  • 研究思路：

1. Integrate exhaustion-state and mitochondrial-fitness logic to define a screenable hypothesis.

2. Use in vivo CRISPR approaches to identify KLHL6 as a candidate node.

3. Dissect how KLHL6 affects TOX stability and mitochondrial dynamics pathways.

  • 转化意义：KLHL6 emerges as a concrete entry point for designing interventions that improve effector persistence in immunotherapy.
  • 局限与下一步：The pharmacologic tractability and long-term safety of manipulating KLHL6 still need to be established.
  • 链接：https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-025-09926-8

Targeting excessive cholesterol deposition alleviates secondary lymphoedema

  • 期刊 / 日期：Nature / 2026-02-11
  • 研究问题：Is abnormal cholesterol accumulation merely a by-product of lymphoedema, or does it actively drive tissue remodeling and disease severity?
  • 模型 / 队列：Human lymphoedema samples plus two mouse models with intervention experiments.
  • 关键方法：Human tissue analysis, lymphatic-flow correction, and cyclodextrin-based cholesterol depletion in mice.
  • 主要内容：The study reframes secondary lymphoedema as a disorder of failed sterol clearance after lymphatic drainage impairment.
  • 核心发现：Excess cholesterol deposition promoted pathological remodeling, while cholesterol depletion improved tissue state and lymphatic regeneration in mice.
  • 研究思路：

1. Verify cholesterol accumulation in human lymphoedema tissue.

2. Link improved lymphatic drainage to reduced cholesterol burden.

3. Show in mouse models that lowering cholesterol changes disease outcome rather than merely marking it.

  • 转化意义：The work identifies a metabolic disease mechanism in a condition usually framed structurally, opening new therapeutic hypotheses.
  • 局限与下一步：Long-term feasibility, dosage strategy, and safety in human patients remain unresolved.
  • 链接：https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-025-10016-y
